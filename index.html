WELCOME TO MURDARP

Explore rules, join factions, and connect via Discord

Player Owned Businesses

Players can launch and manage their own businesses—from nightclubs and mechanic shops to law firms and corner stores—shaping the city’s economy with their vision. Set your own prices, hire staff, customize interiors, and build a reputation as the go-to spot in town. Whether you're running legit operations or fronting something shady, the city is yours to build.

Law Enforcement

Uphold the law and protect the city by joining one of several elite agencies: LSPD, State Troopers, Park Rangers, or the FIB. Each department offers unique responsibilities—from street patrols and traffic stops to federal investigations and wilderness enforcement. Rise through the ranks, specialize in tactical units or detective work, and shape the justice system your way.

Criminal Underworld

Step into the shadows and carve your legacy in the city’s criminal underworld. Whether you're running a street crew, laundering money through a nightclub, or coordinating high-stakes heists with a cartel, the choices—and risks—are yours. Build networks, control turf, and rise through the ranks by outsmarting law enforcement and rival factions. From petty hustles to organized crime empires, the city rewards those bold enough to bend the rules and rewrite the game.
